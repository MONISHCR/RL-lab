Download and Upload Instructions
How to Download Files in Google Colab
  1. Click on the respective "Download" button for the experiment.
  2. The file will automatically start downloading to your computer.
How to Upload Files in Google Colab
  1. Go to your Google Colab notebook.
  2. Click on the "Files" icon on the left sidebar.
  3. Click the "Upload" button and select the file you want to upload.
How to Change Your Roll Number
  1. Find the variable named roll_number in your code.
  2. Change the value to your new roll number: 21BD1A66XX-RL-EXPT-XX.
Sharing Your Colab File

Make sure that your Google Colab file's sharing settings are set to "Anyone with the link can view", not "Restricted".
